Pepper

Pepper was introduced in Tokyo on 5 June 2014 by Masayoshi Son, founder of SoftBank.[1] and was showcased in SoftBank Mobile phone stores in Japan beginning the next day.[2][3] Pepper's ability to recognize emotion is based on detection and analysis of facial expressions and voice tones.
